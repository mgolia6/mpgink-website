What it is

It started in 2018 as a way to connect with a new team — one email, every Friday, no exceptions. It never stopped.

Each edition connects disparate things: people born and lost on that date in history, a word worth keeping, a visual puzzle, and a personal story that ties it all together. Somehow, every week, the threads meet in the middle — and you head into the weekend with a little more perspective than you had before.
