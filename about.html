Come on in

This is where ideas learn to breathe.

Words, images, stories, sounds, software — anything you can imagine finds form here. mpgink is a creative collective: one founder, an AI build partner, and a workshop with the doors open. And maybe, in watching things come to life, you'll feel the pull to make something of your own.

01

Why this exists.

mpgink exists to create, share, and celebrate the act of making things. Not because they're perfect — because they're alive.

Creativity is contagious. Sharing it is the spark. Whether it's a beat, a design, a story, a prototype, or a ridiculous idea that might just work — if it brings joy or lights something up in someone else, it's worth putting into the world.

This is the studio. The doors are open.

02

The world we're reaching for.

A world where creativity isn't gatekept, overthought, or reserved for serious artists. Where kids build things because they're curious, and adults make things because they remember what that feels like.

Where communities form around the simple act of saying: hey, I made this — check it out.

Lo-fi mindset. Infinite ambition. Wonder and experimentation living side by side. The goal was never perfection. It was always connection.

03

How we move.

Create without permission.
You don't need credentials or anyone's approval. If you have an idea, make it. If it sucks, make another one.
Joy over perfection.
The point is to feel something while you're making it — and let someone else feel something when they see it.
Be a catalyst, not a guru.
We're not here to tell you how to create. We're here to get so excited about making things it becomes contagious.
Make space for wonder.
Creativity thrives in curiosity, play, and the "what if?" mindset. Wonder is the engine.
Community is the outcome.
Art isn't just for the artist. The magic happens when someone connects with what you made — even just one person.
Stay lo-fi, infinite in ambition.
Low pressure, high output. No gatekeeping. Just making.
04

Where it all came from.

Early 2000s
It started with a name and a feeling. mpgink had no real direction — just a love for the sound of it and a need to make something. Writing came first. Stories as a kid, worlds built on paper, characters pulled from Berkeley Breathed's Opus. Poetry. Short stories. Whatever needed to get out.
2009
The voice found rhythm. Writing became rap and spoken word — memorizing the work, performing it, feeling what it meant to put words in the air. Around the same time, the visual side woke up: logos, characters, a parody t-shirt (Friendlybook, for the Phish heads), posters, photography. No master plan. Just experimenting.
The Blog Years
mattymattemodgepodge gave it a home. A place for the writing that had been living in endless Word docs. It worked, for a while. Then life happened. It's still out there — dormant, not dead. The pull to return is real.
The Invention Phase
Ideas that wanted to be things. The VacBat — a battery pack for corded vacuums, ahead of its time. A TV pilot called Pilot. Beats in Ableton. Piano aspirations. App concepts: Lyric Nest (social media for writers), a Phish show scorecard. For years, most of them stayed ideas. All of them mattered.
2026 — The Collective
The ideas started shipping. That Phish show scorecard? It's Phreezer — live, with a community of phans rating shows in real time. The experiments became Job Odyssey and One Percent. An Etsy store. A son writing his own books. AI closed the gap between vision and execution: one founder and an AI build partner, run like a full product org. mpgink stopped being just a name and became a collective — mission · passion · gratitude. Not a portfolio. A workshop with the doors open.

If something here stirred you, follow it.

mpgink is a catalyst. The rest is yours.
