Arlo & Ash Explorers Series

Arlo and the Million-Mile Truck — by Arlo Golia (with a little help from dad).

Arlo loves trucks — big ones, important ones, and the ones with stories to tell. When he visits a real fleet shop with his dad, he discovers a million-mile truck and the fleet it belongs to. Through real photographs and a kid's-eye view, Arlo explores how trucks work, why safety matters, and what it takes to keep these machines on the road. A true-to-life adventure for young readers who are curious about engines, tools, and the world around them.
