# mpgink Brand + Copy — source of truth (2026-07-21)

## Positioning
- **mpgink** — a creative collective founded by Matthew Golia. Always lowercase.
- The name is the operating system: **m**ission · **p**assion · **g**ratitude.
- Honest frame: one founder + an AI build partner, run like a full product org —
  one operating standard, automated quality gates and daily canaries, a
  mission-control dashboard keeping the portfolio honest.
- Voice: direct, conversational, lo-fi, anti-perfectionist. "Built lo-fi.
  Shipped fast. Iterated in public." No corporate fluff, ever.

## Tagline (chosen for LinkedIn)
> a creative collective — mission · passion · gratitude · building AI-augmented
> products people actually use

## Publication guardrails (hard rules)
1. **Personal/health and family-internal products are NOT featured on the
   public site** — no naming the wellness instrument or the family condo app.
   "Family tools" as a category mention is the ceiling. Arlowise may be named
   as "reading apps for my kids" without kids' details.
2. **No user counts or revenue numbers** unless Matthew explicitly approves
   the specific number.
3. Copy Matthew hasn't reviewed doesn't ship — draft, flag, wait.
