The job search is a grind.
Make it an odyssey.

Job Odyssey is an AI-powered job-search platform. It scrapes fresh openings and scores every one against your actual résumé and your stated goals — so you spend your energy on the roles worth chasing, with materials tailored to land them.

How the odyssey works

Four stages, one compass: fit to your goals, not just keyword overlap.

Bring your résumé
Upload it, tell the platform where you're headed — target roles, industries, dealbreakers. That becomes the compass everything is measured against.
Openings come to you
Fresh roles are scraped daily and scored 0–100 on an anchored rubric — fit to your experience, skills, and goals, with honest gaps called out.
Triage and tailor
Move the strong fits into your pipeline. Generate a tailored résumé, cover letter, and gap guide for each one — built from your real materials.
Track to the offer
Applications move through a real pipeline — applied, screening, interviewing, offer — with interview practice and coaching along the way.
Fit scores that mean something
One anchored rubric defines what a 90 versus a 60 actually means, so scores are comparable across every opening. Pay never penalizes, and gaps are honest — only the material ones, most critical first.
Materials that match the job
Tailored résumés and cover letters generated from your real experience for each specific role — plus a gap guide for what to address before the interview.
A real pipeline
Every application tracked from research to offer. No more spreadsheet archaeology at week six.
Coaching built in
Interview practice with feedback, a weekly briefing on your momentum, and an AI compass that knows your goals — growing into a full career-coaching marketplace.
